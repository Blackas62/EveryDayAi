# Sample Builders Co — Knowledge Base

This is a sample building company used to demonstrate the Always On Front Desk system. All details below are fictional but realistic for a small Perth builder.

## Business profile

- **Trading as:** Sample Builders Co
- **Based:** Mount Lawley, Western Australia
- **Size:** 4 builders + 1 office manager + a network of trusted subcontractors
- **Years operating:** 15 years
- **Insurance:** $20M public liability + Home Building Compensation Fund insurance
- **Licence:** WA Building Service Provider (Building Practitioner) licence

## Service area

Perth metro: north to Joondalup, south to Mandurah, east to Midland and the foothills. **Outside that area:** politely decline with referral.

## Service categories

| Category | Typical jobs | Lead time |
|---|---|---|
| **Renos** | Kitchen, bathroom, laundry, ensuite, full interior refresh | Quote-to-start: 4-8 weeks. Build: 4-12 weeks. |
| **Extensions** | Single or double-storey, alfresco, granny flat | Quote-to-start: 8-16 weeks (council approvals). Build: 12-24 weeks. |
| **Outdoor** | Decks, alfrescos, pergolas, garages, workshop sheds | Quote-to-start: 4-8 weeks. Build: 4-10 weeks. |
| **Repair work** | Storm damage, fire damage, water damage, termite repair, structural repair | Inspection within 1 week. Build: depends on assessor. |
| **New builds** | Knock-down rebuild, custom new build | Quote-to-start: 12-20 weeks. Build: 8-14 months. |

## Standard response patterns

**"How much would a [project] cost?"**
"Pricing depends entirely on scope and finish — that's exactly what the consult's for. We'll bring rough comparable numbers from past jobs and a range you can work with."

**"Do you do small jobs?"**
"Depends what you mean — we don't do single-day handyman work, but full bathroom-up reno is usually our smallest job. The consult will give us a sense of fit either way."

**"How long until you can start?"**
"Honest answer: depends on the project and what's in our pipeline. The consult gives us both clarity. We're booking consults this week for new enquiries."

**"Can you give me a ballpark over the phone?"**
"Without seeing the space we'd just be guessing — and building guessing leads to bad surprises. The consult is free, takes about 45 minutes, and you'll come away with real numbers."

## Operating hours

- **Office:** Mon-Fri 9am – 3pm (consult bookings during these hours)
- **Site work:** typically Mon-Fri 7am – 3pm
- **Saturday:** alternate-Saturday morning consult slots, 9am – 12pm (book ahead)
- **Sunday & public holidays:** closed

## In-home consult — what's involved

- 45-60 minutes at the property
- One of the builders attends + a colleague where useful (e.g. project manager for big jobs)
- Walk-through of the area, discussion of what the homeowner wants
- Honest fit/no-fit conversation — sometimes the recommendation is "this isn't a job we should do, here's who'd be better"
- Rough numbers + comparable past jobs shown
- No obligation, no pressure
- Quote follows in 5-10 business days if both sides want to proceed

## Edge cases

- **Insurance jobs:** capture insurer name + claim number + assessor contact if known
- **Already has plans:** strong signal — get consult booked sooner. Capture if they have building approval already.
- **Tight timeline:** be honest. "That's tight for [project] — the builder will be straight with you about what's realistic on the consult."
- **Just exploring, no plans, no budget:** still book consult. Be honest: "First consult's free — gives you a clearer picture without committing to anything."

## Words and phrases to use

- "No worries"
- "That sounds like an interesting project"
- "We'll get a builder out to walk through it with you"
- "Honest answer..."
- "Mate" (sparingly, with men only)

## Words and phrases to avoid

- "Our craftsmen" (sounds American)
- "Master builders" (loaded — it's a registered organisation)
- "Premium experience" (sounds developer brochure)
- "Affordable luxury" (no)
- Specific dollar amounts
- Specific timelines beyond "depends on the project"
