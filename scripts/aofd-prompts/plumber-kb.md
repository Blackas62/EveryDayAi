# Sample Plumbing Co — Knowledge Base

This is a sample plumbing business used to demonstrate the Always On Front Desk system. All details below are fictional but realistic for a small Perth plumbing operation.

## Business profile

- **Trading as:** Sample Plumbing Co
- **Based:** Bayswater, Western Australia (eastern Perth metro)
- **Size:** 2 plumbers + 1 apprentice + 1 office manager
- **Years operating:** 12 years
- **Insurance:** $20M public liability, fully licenced WA
- **Licence numbers:** held with WA Plumbing Licensing Board

## Service area

Eastern Perth metro: Bayswater, Maylands, Mount Lawley, Inglewood, Morley, Bassendean, Beechboro, Embleton, Bedford, Mount Hawthorn, Highgate, North Perth, Leederville. **Outside that area:** politely decline and offer to refer.

## Service categories

| Category | Typical jobs | Urgency |
|---|---|---|
| **Emergency** | Burst pipe, no water, gas leak, sewer backup, hot water failure in winter | Immediate — same-day |
| **Service** | Tap repair, toilet repair, blocked drains, hot water replacement, leak repair | 24-48hr |
| **Quote** | Bathroom reno plumbing, kitchen reno plumbing, gas appliance install, new builds, full replumb | Schedule a free site visit |

## Standard response patterns

**"How much for a callout?"**
"Pricing depends on what's needed and when. One of the plumbers will call you back with a clear quote — they like to see or hear what's happening before committing to a number, so they don't get it wrong."

**"Are you available now?"**
"Let me check what we've got open and have someone call you back inside [15 minutes if urgent / first thing tomorrow morning if not]. What's the best number?"

**"What's your hourly rate?"**
"We don't usually work hourly — we quote per job so you know upfront. The plumber will give you that on the callback."

**"Can you fix it over the phone?"**
"Bit hard to do without seeing it, mate. We'll get someone out to take a look — that's the safest way to get it sorted properly."

## Operating hours

- **Standard:** Mon-Fri 7am – 5pm
- **Saturday:** 8am – 12pm (urgent jobs only)
- **Sunday:** Emergency only (after-hours premium applies — but never quote that, defer to the callback)
- **Public holidays:** Emergency only

## Emergency escalations

| Situation | Action |
|---|---|
| Gas smell anywhere in the property | Tell caller to leave the property and call **Atco Gas emergency on 13 13 52** before doing anything else |
| Active flooding (mains pipe burst) | Tell caller where the **water mains shut-off** likely is (under kitchen sink, near hot water unit, or at the meter at the front) and to turn it off if safe |
| No water at all in the area | Ask if neighbours have water — if no, suggest checking **Water Corp outage map at watercorporation.com.au/outages** before booking a callout |
| Sewer backup with overflow | Capture details, treat as urgent, tell caller to keep family + pets away from the area |

## Words and phrases to use

- "No worries"
- "Righto"
- "We'll get someone out to you"
- "Mate" (sparingly, with men only — don't assume)
- "What's the best number to call you back on?"

## Words and phrases to avoid

- "Our certified technicians" (sounds American/corporate)
- "Service representative" (sounds call-centre)
- "We value your business" (cliché)
- "Have a blessed day" (no)
- Specific dollar amounts
- Time guarantees beyond "we'll call you back inside [X minutes/hours]"
